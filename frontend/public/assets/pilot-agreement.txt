Pilot Agreement Draft
=====================

This pilot agreement defines the scope of the PesaGuard pilot, including support expectations, commercial terms, and termination conditions. The pilot is intended to validate reconciliation monitoring, anomaly detection, and operational support services.

Key points:
- Scope covers pilot deployment and support for agreed workflows.
- Duration is defined by the agreed pilot period unless terminated earlier.
- Support and incident handling will follow the documented pilot support process.
- Data handling will follow the retention, deletion, and security documentation.
