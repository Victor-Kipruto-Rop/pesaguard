PesaGuard Support Readiness Brief
================================

Support channels:
- Email: pilot-support@pesaguard.example
- Priority incident channel: #pesaguard-pilot
- Status portal: /status

Response expectations:
- Critical: 15 minutes
- High: 1 hour
- Medium: 4 hours

Operational notes:
- Incident reports should include tenant context, affected workflow, and relevant transaction identifiers.
- Support workflows are aligned with the incident response and service status experience.
