## Frequently Asked Questions (Pilot)

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: PesaGuard supports a documented residency posture for primary data, backups, and logs. We will confirm the selected deployment region for your tenant during onboarding and enforce residency controls as configured.

Q: Can I change the language?
A: Yes. Each tenant can set a default language at the tenant level; individual users may override the preference where supported. During the pilot we support English and Kiswahili translations for core workflows and alerts.

Q: What alerts are sent?
A: Alerts are delivered to the channels configured for your tenant (Slack, SMS, and optionally Email). Content is localized according to the tenant's preferred locale.

Q: Does PesaGuard support Kenya-specific formatting?
A: Yes. Dashboards and exported reports use Kenya-appropriate date/time (EAT) and currency (KES) formatting when the tenant region is set to Kenya.

Q: How do I get support during the pilot?
A: We provide a shared Slack channel for pilot participants and a named support contact. See the pilot runbook for escalation steps and SLAs.

Q: Who signs off residency and legal terms?
A: Our legal reviewer will review the residency decision document and produce a signed readiness statement before we publish the pilot status page.
