# Getting Started with PesaGuard

PesaGuard helps finance and operations teams monitor M-Pesa reconciliation exceptions in near real time.

## What you can expect
- Review discrepancies and alert routes from a central dashboard.
- Keep payment operations aligned with your SLA and audit needs.
- Configure tenant-specific alerting, retention, and residency settings.

## First steps
1. Connect your reconciliation source and verify the tenant identifier.
2. Review the alert routing and notification channels for your tenant.
3. Confirm your preferred language and region settings.
4. Share the dashboard with your operations team.

## Support
If you need help, contact your implementation team or review the FAQ for common questions.
