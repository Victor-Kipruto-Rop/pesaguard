# Legal Review Checklist

## Documents to review
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Data Processing Agreement
- Pilot Agreement

## Review questions
- Is the scope of services accurate for the pilot?
- Are data retention and deletion obligations clear?
- Are incident notification obligations appropriate?
- Does the draft align with the intended pilot customer model?
- Are there any compliance or regulatory obligations specific to the customer or jurisdiction?

## Approval workflow
1. Share the draft documents with a qualified legal reviewer.
2. Capture comments and redlines.
3. Update the documents.
4. Obtain written sign-off.
5. Publish the approved versions to the pilot environment.
