## Roles
PesaGuard acts as a service provider processing customer transaction and reconciliation data on behalf of the customer.

## Security obligations
PesaGuard will implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the data processed.

## Incident handling
PesaGuard will notify the customer of material security incidents affecting the service as soon as reasonably practicable.

## Deletion and return
On request or upon termination, PesaGuard will assist with export and deletion of customer data in accordance with the retention and offboarding policy.

## Governing law
This draft is intended for review by qualified legal counsel and should be finalized before use with production customers.
