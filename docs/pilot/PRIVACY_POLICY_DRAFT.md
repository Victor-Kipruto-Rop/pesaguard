## Overview
PesaGuard processes transaction and reconciliation data to provide monitoring, anomaly detection, and operational reporting services.

## Data collected
We may collect transaction metadata, account identifiers, anomaly records, and support communications necessary to operate the service.

## Use of data
Data is used to provide reconciliation, alerting, incident support, and service improvement.

## Retention and deletion
Customer data is retained according to the PesaGuard retention policy and may be deleted following the documented offboarding process.

## Security
PesaGuard applies access controls, logging, rate limiting, and backup practices to protect customer data.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact the PesaGuard operations team.
