# Pilot Support Channel

## Primary support route
- Shared Slack channel: `#pesaguard-pilot`
- Email: `pilot-support@pesaguard.example`
- On-call contact: designated pilot operator

## Response expectations
- Critical issues: immediate attention
- High priority: within 30 minutes
- Standard requests: during business hours

## Escalation
If a pilot customer is materially affected, escalate to the incident lead and customer contact immediately.
