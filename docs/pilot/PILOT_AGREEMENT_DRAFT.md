## Parties
This pilot agreement is entered into by PesaGuard and the pilot customer for the purpose of evaluating the PesaGuard reconciliation service.

## Scope
The pilot covers M-Pesa reconciliation, anomaly detection, and operational support for the agreed pilot period.

## Duration
The pilot shall remain in effect for the agreed evaluation period unless terminated earlier by either party.

## Fees
Fees, if any, shall be set out in a separate schedule or waived during the pilot period.

## Support
PesaGuard will provide support through the pilot support channel and documented incident response procedures.

## Data handling
The parties agree to follow the data retention, deletion, and security practices described in the operational documentation.

## Termination
Either party may terminate the pilot with reasonable notice.
