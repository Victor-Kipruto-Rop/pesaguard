# PesaGuard Legal and Commercial Readiness Draft

## 1. Terms of Service
- Define the service scope, acceptable use, and customer responsibilities.
- Clarify that PesaGuard provides reconciliation assistance and is not a substitute for regulated financial advice.

## 2. Privacy Policy
- Describe what transaction and customer data is collected.
- Explain data retention, deletion, and security practices.

## 3. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
- Define PesaGuard as a processor for customer data where applicable.
- Clarify incident response and breach notification expectations.

## 4. ODPC / data protection review
- Confirm whether the current operating model requires ODPC registration.
- Document the review outcome with a qualified legal advisor.

## 5. Liability / E&O
- Review whether errors or omissions coverage is appropriate before handling production traffic for paying customers.

## 6. Pricing and pilot agreement
- Draft a simple pilot agreement covering scope, duration, fees, and termination.
- Agree on pricing model before onboarding the first paid pilot customer.

## 7. Support and SLA
- Define a support channel and response expectations.
- Document an initial SLA suitable for pilot customers.

## 8. Onboarding and offboarding checklist
- Include connector setup, tenant config, alert routing, and shadow-mode validation.
- Tie offboarding to the retention and deletion policy.

## 9. Status page
- Publish a lightweight public status page or incident page for customer visibility.
